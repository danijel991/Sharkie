Instructions


Be the shark, face the dangers and defeat Orqi, the evil orca that makes his nuisance in the deep waters. Use the fin attack to beat the opponents with Sharkie. When you have collected the poison bottles, you can also shoot bubbles.
Usw arrow keys to move Sharkie
Arrow left - move left
Arrow right - move right
Arrow up - move up
Arrow down - move down
Space - Fins attack
D - Shoot bubbles
